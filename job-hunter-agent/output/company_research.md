# Company Overview  
**Company Name:** Beta Inc  
**Location:** New York, NY (Hybrid)  
**Founded:** 2015  
**Size:** ~250 employees (201–500)  
**Industry:** Marketing & Advertising, Digital Analytics, CRM Solutions  
**Core Offerings:**  
- Data-driven marketing strategies  
- Customer segmentation and targeting  
- Campaign performance analytics  
- Proprietary ad-tech platform **BetaAnalytics**  

# Mission and Values  
**Mission:** “Empower brands through actionable insights and data-driven marketing.”  
**Values:**  
- *Innovation* – Continuous improvement of tools and techniques  
- *Collaboration* – Cross-functional teamwork between data, marketing, and sales  
- *Integrity* – Commitment to ethical handling of data  
- *Customer-Centricity* – Putting client success at the core of every solution  

# Recent News or Changes  
- **Series B Funding (June 2023):** $20 M raised to scale the **BetaAnalytics** platform (TechCrunch).  
- **Acquisition (March 2024):** AI startup “DataWise” acquired to bolster predictive modeling and machine learning capabilities.  
- **Product Launch (April 2024):** Inauguration of **Beta Insights Lab** blog for sharing analytics best practices and case studies.

# Role Context and Product Involvement  
**Position:** Junior Data Analyst  
**Team:**  
- Reports to Senior Analytics Manager  
- Works closely with marketing and sales teams, as well as data engineering  
- Part of a 5-person analytics pod embedded in the Digital Strategy group  

**Key Responsibilities:**  
- *Data Cleaning & Validation* – Standardize and enrich raw campaign and CRM data  
- *Ad-Hoc Reporting* – Build dashboards and one-off analyses for marketing/sales initiatives  
- *Collaboration* – Translate data insights into recommendations for campaign optimization  

**Product Context:**  
- You’ll analyze data generated by the **BetaAnalytics** platform and CRM systems (likely Salesforce)  
- Support A/B testing and attribution modeling for digital ad campaigns  
- Contribute to analytics frameworks that feed into machine-learning models from the recent DataWise integration  

# Likely Interview Topics  
- **Technical Skills**  
  - SQL: querying large datasets, joins, window functions  
  - Python: pandas data manipulation, data cleaning best practices  
  - Excel: pivot tables, VLOOKUP/XLOOKUP, data visualization basics  
- **Data Fundamentals**  
  - Data quality checks: deduplication, handling nulls/outliers  
  - Basic statistics: mean, median, standard deviation  
- **Analytics & Reporting**  
  - Designing ad-hoc dashboards (e.g., in Tableau or Looker)  
  - KPI definitions: CAC, LTV, conversion rate, ROI  
  - Storytelling: translating numbers into business recommendations  
- **Cross-Functional Collaboration**  
  - Communicating findings to non-technical stakeholders  
  - Incorporating feedback from marketing/sales into analysis  
- **Behavioral Questions**  
  - Problem-solving: Describe a time you dealt with messy data  
  - Teamwork: Example of collaborating with another department
